What clients say

Why choose Sidekick Sites?

What do you do each month?
  • Keep your landing page fast, tidy and up to date
  • Manage paid social (Meta/Instagram; TikTok optional)
  • Test creatives/offers, shift budget to winners
  • Track leads (UTMs, page URL, timestamps) and inbox alerts
  • Send a plain-English weekly summary + monthly roll-up
Who owns what?

You own your domain, brand assets, and ad accounts. We run everything for you.

We provide the site and creative templates on a managed license while you’re subscribed. Want to take the site with you later? There’s a simple buyout option for a full handover.

How does billing work?

One-time setup fee to launch; then a monthly management fee. Your ad spend is paid directly to the platforms (you keep control of budgets). We suggest a 3-month minimum to get meaningful learnings, then month-to-month.

What results should I expect?

Our job is to increase qualified enquiries, not just clicks. We measure:

  • Leads per week/month and lead source
  • Cost per lead from ads
  • Page contact-rate (visits → enquiries)

We start lean, then iterate based on data.

Do you outsource?

We’re your single point of contact. If we bring in specialists (e.g., video editor), they work under our processes and quality bar. You’ll still get one clear plan and one invoice.

Can I pause or cancel?

Yes—after the initial term, it’s month-to-month. You can pause ads or the plan with 14 days’ notice. If you want a full site handover, choose the buyout option.
