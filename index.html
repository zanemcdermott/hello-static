Why choose Sidekick Sites?

What exactly do you deliver?

A fast, focused landing page with a frictionless contact flow wired to your inbox. We can add:

  • Optional Airtable lead log
  • Paid social setup (Meta/IG; TikTok optional)
  • Basic SEO (titles, meta, share cards, sitemap)
How fast can we launch?

Usually days, not weeks. We start simple, ship, and iterate. You send must-have copy/photos; we can use clean placeholders to go live quickly.

Do I own everything?

You own your data (leads, domains, ad accounts). We run this as a managed monthly service; if you ever want to take it in-house, we hand over cleanly.

How do we track leads & results?

Email notifications immediately; optional Airtable dashboard with source (UTMs), page URL, and timestamps. For ads, we report weekly in plain English.
